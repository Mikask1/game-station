Block Stacker

Block Stacker is a challenging and addictive game where your goal is to stack blocks without allowing them to go over the previous block. Your precision and timing are crucial in achieving a high score and building the tallest tower possible.

Traffic Run

In this thrilling game, your driving skills will be put to the test as you navigate through a challenging road shaped like the number 8. Your objective is simple but demanding: avoid crashing into other vehicle and complete as many laps as possible without any collisions.
